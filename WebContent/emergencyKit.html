Basic Disaster Supplies Kit

Note: Store items in airtight plastic bags and put your entire disaster supplies kit in one or two easy-to-carry containers such as plastic bins or a duffel bag.

Water

One gallon per person per day for several days (for drinking and sanitation)

Food

At least a several-day supply of non-perishable food

Radio

Battery-powered or hand crank radio and a NOAA Weather Radio with tone alert

Flashlight

Essential for power outages

First Aid Kit

Basic medical supplies

Extra Batteries

For all battery-powered devices

Whistle

To signal for help

Dust Mask

To help filter contaminated air

Shelter Items

Plastic sheeting, scissors and duct tape (to shelter in place)

Sanitation

Moist towelettes, garbage bags and plastic ties (for personal sanitation)

Tools

Wrench or pliers (to turn off utilities)

Can Opener

Manual can opener (for food)

Local Maps

Physical maps of your area

Cell Phone

With chargers and a backup battery
