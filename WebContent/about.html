About FloodAlert India

FloodAlert India is a national initiative dedicated to saving lives and reducing flood-related damages through real-time monitoring, early warning systems, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely flood warnings and empower communities with the knowledge and tools needed to prepare for and respond to flood emergencies across India.

Our Vision

To create a flood-resilient India where no life is lost to preventable flood disasters, and communities can thrive despite increasing climate challenges.
